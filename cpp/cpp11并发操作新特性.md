> c++ 11 标准引入了四个头文件来支持多线程编程

- <atomic>
  
  - std::atomic
  
  - std::atomic_flag

- <thread>
  
  - std::thread
  
  - std::this_thread

- <mutex>
  
  - std::mutex
  
  - std::lock_guard
  
  - std::unique_lock

- <condition_variable>
  
  - std::condition_variable
  
  - std::condition_variable_any

- <future>
  
  - std::promise
  
  - std::package_task
  
  - std::future
  
  - std::shared_future
  
  - std::async() 函数
